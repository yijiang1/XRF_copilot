## Questions for XRF Simulation Setup

1. What is the full path to the ground truth objects file (.npy format)?
2. What elements do you want to simulate? (e.g., Cu, Ca, Fe)
3. What is the beam energy in keV?
4. What is the incident probe intensity?
5. Do you want to include self-absorption in the simulation?
6. Do you want to include probe attenuation in the simulation?
7. What is the physical size of the sample volume in cm?
8. What is the diameter of the detector in cm?
9. What is the distance from the sample to the detector in cm?
10. What is the spacing between detector pixels in cm?
11. Do you want to apply any 3D rotation? If yes, what are the rotation angles (x, y, z) in degrees?
